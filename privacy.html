PKDB Page Capture - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

What this extension does

PKDB Page Capture converts the current browser tab to a PDF and uploads it to your personal Google Drive.

Data collected

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It does not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

Google Drive access

The extension requests access to Google Drive with the drive.file scope, which limits access to only files created by the extension. It cannot read, modify, or delete any other files in your Drive. Authentication is handled entirely by Chrome's built-in identity API.

Data storage

No data is stored locally or remotely by the extension. PDFs are uploaded directly to your Google Drive account.

Third parties

No data is shared with third parties. The only external service contacted is the Google Drive API for file uploads.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
